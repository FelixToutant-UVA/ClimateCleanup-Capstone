About Us

We are a team of six Computational Social Science students from the University of Amsterdam, passionate about creating positive change in our food systems. This website is the result of our bachelor thesis project, where we developed a digital platform to connect food forests with food businesses. Our goal is to support sustainable, regenerative agriculture by making it easier for businesses and consumers to access and understand the true value of food grown in harmony with nature. Through this project, we hope to inspire a shift toward healthier food choices that benefit both people and the planet.
